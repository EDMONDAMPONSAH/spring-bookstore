A guard ring is a technique used in electrical engineering to achieve equal potential distribution and improve the efficiency of string insulators, especially in high voltage applications. String insulators are used to support overhead power transmission lines and maintain a safe distance between the conductors and the ground. Here's how the use of a guard ring accomplishes these goals: A bus bar is a metallic strip which conducts electricity by serving as a common connection point for multiple electrical circuits. ng uniform potential distribution with the help of a guard ring, the occurrence of corona discharge and partial discharge is minimized. This leads to reduced energy losses and improved efficiency of the transmission line. Additionally, by reducing the stress on individual insulators, the guard ring helps to extend their lifespan, thereby enhancing the overall reliability of the system. In summary, the use of a guard ring in a string of suspension insulators ensures equal potential distribution along the insulator string, thereby reducing the risk of insulator flashover and improving the efficiency and reliability of the transmission line.
